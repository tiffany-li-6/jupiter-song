“I Vow To Thee Our Savior”

“I Vow To Thee Our Savior”

By Tiffany Li, Parody of "I Vow To Thee My Country"

Lyrics

I vow to thee, our savior, all the love we can give

For saving and reviving, the dear song of England

The love that saw the before, the love that knows present

That thanks him for his service, for saving from the drought

Sharing “Jupiter” to the world, and claiming English reign

The love that stays unwavering through all of time

 

And there's another reason, for he’s shaped music now

His sound heard strongly in greats, the classics we all love

Was leading the postmodern, music from the future

Now loved by all the public, influencing big stars

A trailblazer, giving classical a great fame and love

Transforming a name for this music, we thank with all our heart

 

 

Author Commentary

My cover with rewritten lyrics for “I Vow To Thee My Country” serves as a last closing statement to express the truly grand impact and public support that Gustav Holst has. In 1921, Holst rearranged his piece “Jupiter” of The Planets for the British anthem, which received wide love and recognition over the years (Linton 2022). My present rendition, made just over a century later, acts as a commemorative work that highlights Holsts’s lifetime achievements and also shows how Holst’s works from decades ago have not only transcended the bounds of classical music from his time, but also lasted in reception and influence into modern society and in music that is written now. A hope for  this cover is to package all of society’s sentiments, praise, and gratitude towards Holst, into a more consumer-friendly form of media to engage in and to leave remembering.
